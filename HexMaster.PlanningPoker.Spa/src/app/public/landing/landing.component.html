ScrumPoker

Planning Poker

Planning poker, also called Scrum poker, is a consensus-based, gamified technique for estimating, mostly used to estimate effort or relative size of development goals in software development.

We take away the pain of having to buy planning poker cards and allow you to poker digitally. You can immidiately start pokering, or define an entire refinement session.
